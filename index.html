About Me

EN DE FR

My name is Amy, and I am an experienced pet-sitter based in Friedrichshain. I grew up with a large variety of animal friends (mostly fluffy, but also scaly, woolly, hooved - all sorts) and have been making friends professionally since 2021.

I love the challenge of adapting to each animal's unique personality and needs, and establishing a bond based on trust and reliability, with both furbaby and pet parent.
